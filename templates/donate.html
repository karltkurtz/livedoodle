// The story
What if strangers on the internet could draw on a screen in my house? A weekend of tinkering later and it actually worked — two Raspberry Pis, a WebSocket server, and a camera pointed at a 7" display. The latency turned out way lower than expected. Watching the first stroke show up live on real hardware was one of the most satisfying moments I've had building something.
